EcoApps Hack

Developing Apps for the High School Eco-System

Description

Welcome to EcoApps Hack, an exciting hackathon focused on developing innovative applications for the high school eco-system. Join us as we bring together talented developers, designers, and problem solvers to create apps that cater to the unique needs of high school students, teachers, and parents.

During this one-day event, participants will have the opportunity to collaborate, ideate, and build functional prototypes or minimum viable products (MVPs) that can enhance the educational experience, promote sustainability, and improve communication within the high school eco-system.

Join us on August 15, 2023, at the Auditorium of Notre Dame High School in San Jose for an unforgettable day of coding, innovation, and teamwork. Get ready to develop solutions that empower high school students, facilitate effective teaching methods, and engage parents in their children's education. Let's make a positive impact together!
